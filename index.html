There are so many Ultraman series and specials, that it is hard to keep them apart. They all follow a central plot line, however, in which the Science Patrol must defend Earth from sinister monsters and aliens, by training a team of less sinister monsters and aliens. Fun fact: many of the monster costumes were reused multiple times, but spray painted different colours to appear different.

The Ultraman television show first aired in 1966, and is on-going. At the time of writing, there have been 37 Ultraman series, 28 Ultraman movies, three television specials, three animated specials, three shorts, and twelve video games.

This is not unique in Japan, a country which has a history of creating exceptionally long-running children's television programs.
